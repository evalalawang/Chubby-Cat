Welcome, tea lover!

We let Nature speak for itself. Our hand-picked premium organic teas will be your best choice for every occasion.

Organic, hand-picked from nature

In order to bring you the most natrual flavor, we use the finest whole-leaf organic teas. Each leaf is hand-picked on family farms and processed using only traditional methods.

High mountain tea, free from pollution

We source our tea directly from sustainable family-owned tea farms. All tea is tested and verified to be 100% chemical and pesticide free. Growing in high elevation and surrounded by beautiful clouds, our tea comes from the most ideal environment.
